Equipment

For equipment you have 12 slots to fill. 7 armor pieces on the body, 2 rings, 1 amulet, 2 slots for a front bar weapon, and 2 slots for a back bar weapon. When starting, it's important to get all three armor weights on your body to unlock all the passives. 5-1-1 is the most effective layout. Which equipment you use, and in which weight, is dependant on which role you take. I will break down, by role, which equipment would be recommended for starting out.

  • Tank
    1. 5 heavy on the body
    2. 1 medium on shoulder or head
    3. 1 light on head or shoulder
  • Healer
    1. 5 light on body
    2. 1 medium on head or shoulder
    3. 1 heavy on head or shoulder
  • Damage dealer
    1. 5 medium on the body
    2. 1 light on the head or shoulder
    3. 1 heavy on the head or shoulder
For weapons, the most effective options for the supports are:
  1. sword and shield front bar and ice staff back bar for tanks
  2. restoration staff front bar and lightening or ice staff back bar for healers
For damage dealers there are many different options to choose from, but for the purposes of this page I will be focusing on optimum damage output.
  1. For both stamina and magicka dd's, they should use dual wield daggers on the front bar
  2. For stamina dd's the greatsword will offer the most damage output on the back bar
  3. Magicka dd's can utilize the greatsword as well, or if you prefer a more magicka based route you can use a flame staff

equipment Summary

There are many options to fill your 12 slots, and it can get confusing. As long as you have your role picked out you can start to break it down a little easier. these are the most optimum options for a beginner to start with, as you play you'll learn your playstyle and be able to adjust to better suit the group and yourself.
