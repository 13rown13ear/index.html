Alliance Options

When first starting, you will be asked to choose an alliance. This option will determine which "side" of the three banners war you are on. The three options are:

  • Aldmeri Dominion
  • Ebonheart Pact
  • Daggerfall Covenent

Your alliance will also determine where your main quest will take place, and which races are available to you when creating your character.
