Skills and Passives

The skills and passives are available to the player in the form of skill trees. Each class has 3 uniquie skill trees. There are many other's in the game to discover and unlock as well. Unlocking these skills and passives requires skill points, which you can collect through various activities in the game.

  • Skills
    • Weapon and class skill trees have 5 different skills and one ultimate. Although these are where most of your skills will come from they are not the only options available. There are quests you can do to join various guilds within game. Leveling these skill trees will give you 5 more skills and 1 more ultimate per skill tree you level to 10.
  • Passives
    • Passives are always on style buffs you recieve from leveling up your skill trees. It is important to put skill points into your passives to increase the effectivness of your skills. Every skill tree has passives, some trees only have passives, like your racial tree. If you intend to use a skill from a tree it is recommended you level the passives as well to get the most from that skill.

Skill Summary

There are many skill and ultimate options within the game, and your character gets 5 skills and one ultimate per bar, with 2 bars. No matter which role, race, or class you choose, the game has plenty to offer to ensure you get the job done.
