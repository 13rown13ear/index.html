Races

As stated in the alliance section, the choice of alliance will determine which races are available to you in character creation.

Racial Passives

  • Aldmeri Domninion

    • Khajiit

      1. Magicka and stamina recovery
      2. Increased magicka and stamina
      3. Increased critical damage and decreased stealth detection

    • Wood Elf

      1. Increased armor penetration
      2. Increased Stamina Recovery
      3. Increased stamina and decreased disease and poison damage

    • High Elf

      1. Stamina or magicka restoration upon ability activation once every 6 seconds, depending on which is lower
      2. Increased magicka
      3. Increased weapon and spell damage

  • Ebonheart Pact

    • Argonian

      1. Increased healing done
      2. Increased health
      3. Increased magicka and stamina, as well as increased magicka, stamina, and health restoration everytime you drink a potion

    • Dark Elves

      1. Increased magicka and stamina
      2. flame resistance
      3. Increased weapon and spell damage

    • Nord

      1. Increased health
      2. Increased health and ultimate regeneration upon recieving damage
      3. Increased spell and physical resistance

  • Daggerfall Covenant

    • Breton

      1. Increased magicka
      2. Increased spell resistance, (which is doubled if you are afflicted with burning, chilled, or concussed) and increased magicka recovery
      3. Magicka abilities consume less magicka

    • Orc

      1. Increased Stamina
      2. Increased health, as well as health restoration when dealing damage once every 4 seconds
      3. Increased weapon and spell damage and increased sprint speed

    • Redguards

      1. Cost reduction of weapon abilities and reduction in the effectivness of snares applied to you
      2. Increased stamina
      3. Stamina restoration upon dealing damage every 5 seconds

Race Summary

As you can see from the information provided, each race offers up a unique set of passive abilities to unlock as you level up. The affect on the final product is minimal so feel free to choose whichever race you like.
